## 中文

心情不对、犯懒、逃避一件事的时候，脑子里常常是这样的：**知道要做，但情绪上完全不想动，越想越拖。**

这个工具不讲道理、不打鸡血，就是带你走五步，把"卡住"拆解掉：

| 步骤 | 内容 |
|---|---|
| 1️⃣ | 说清楚你现在具体是什么情绪（不是"不好"，是烦躁？恐惧？无力？） |
| 2️⃣ | 说出这个情绪最想让你干嘛（比如刷手机），然后反着来一次 |
| 3️⃣ | 把要做的事压缩成一个两分钟内就能开始的最小动作 |
| 4️⃣ | 用"我选择"造句，自己确认一下（不用保证做到，说出口就行） |
| 5️⃣ | 生成一行记录，方便你复制粘贴留档 |

全程不联网、不用登录、不会有人看到你写了什么——所有内容只存在你自己手机的浏览器里。

**怎么用**

1. 手机浏览器打开 [slowrecover.github.io/unstuck](https://slowrecover.github.io/unstuck/)
2. 浏览器菜单里找"添加到主屏幕"，加个图标，以后像个小 App 一样点开用
3. 卡住的时候点开，跟着五步走一遍就行

**背后的依据**

不是瞎编的，背后是几个真实的心理学研究方向：

> 🧠 **具体命名情绪能降低情绪强度** — affect labeling，Lieberman 的研究
> 🔁 **不压抑冲动，只是不服从它** — DBT 的 opposite action 技术
> ⚡ **行动可以先于动机，不用等感觉对了再做** — 行为激活疗法
> 🧭 **用"我选择"而不是"我必须"，自主感能提高行动的依从性** — 自我决定论，Deci & Ryan

**说明**

- 纯前端页面，没有后端、没有数据库、不发任何网络请求
- 记录只存在浏览器的 localStorage 里，换设备/清缓存就没了，请自己复制保存
- 这是个人在用的小工具，不追求功能齐全，能解决"卡住"这一个问题就够了

## English

When you're avoiding something you know you should do, the pattern is usually the same: **you know what to do, you just don't feel like doing it — and the more you think about it, the longer you stall.**

No pep talk, no lecture. Just five steps to break the stuck moment down:

| Step | What it asks |
|---|---|
| 1️⃣ | Name the exact emotion you're feeling — not "bad," but *which* bad (restless? afraid? drained?) |
| 2️⃣ | Say what that emotion wants you to do (scroll your phone?), then name the opposite action |
| 3️⃣ | Compress the task into the smallest possible action you could start in two minutes |
| 4️⃣ | Confirm with "I choose to..." — no promise required, just say it |
| 5️⃣ | Get a one-line summary you can copy and save |

No network calls, no login, nobody sees what you write — everything lives only in your phone's browser.

**How to use it**

1. Open [slowrecover.github.io/unstuck](https://slowrecover.github.io/unstuck/) on your phone
2. Add it to your home screen from your browser menu — it'll behave like a small standalone app
3. Open it whenever you're stuck, and walk through the five steps

**Why it works**

This isn't made up — it's built on a few real strands of psychological research:

> 🧠 **Naming an emotion specifically lowers its intensity** — affect labeling (Lieberman et al.)
> 🔁 **Don't suppress the urge, just don't obey it** — DBT's opposite action technique
> ⚡ **Action can precede motivation — you don't need to feel ready first** — Behavioral Activation therapy
> 🧭 **"I choose" beats "I must" for follow-through** — Self-Determination Theory (Deci & Ryan)

**Notes**

- Static front-end only — no backend, no database, no network requests
- Entries live in `localStorage` only — switch devices or clear your cache and they're gone, so copy anything worth keeping
- This is a personal tool built to solve exactly one problem — getting unstuck — not a feature-complete app
